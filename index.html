Portfolio

Lulu The Cat and the Fishes of Finora

This project is my ongoing exploration of Unreal Engine 5.5, where I'm developing a strategy RPG focusing on deep gameplay mechanics and narrative-driven experiences. My development efforts center around:

  • Gameplay Ability System (GAS): Implementing core RPG mechanics, skills, and progression systems.
  • Dice-Based Resource Management: Designing and integrating a dice mechanic for strategic decision-making.
  • Fishing Mechanics: Creating fishing gameplay with unique fish behaviors and requirements.
  • Spaceship Navigation: Developing a navigation system to blend space exploration and fishing gameplay seamlessly.
  • UI Design: Designing intuitive UI elements in Figma to support gameplay.
  • 3D Character Modeling and Animation: Creating Lulu the Cat, various fish species, and other characters in Blender.
  • Environment Design: Crafting a dynamic cosmic nebula environment.
  • Custom Asset Integration: Adapting and integrating assets from the Unreal Engine Marketplace to fit the game's vision.

As a solo developer, I'm committed to leveraging UE5's capabilities to create an imaginative and engaging gaming experience. Stay tuned for future builds and updates. itch.io

High Mountain

High Mountain is my first Unreal Engine 5 learning project, in which I explored and implemented a variety of core gameplay mechanics and engine features. I developed this third-person action RPG prototype to gain hands-on experience with:

  • Motion Warping
  • MetaSounds
  • Linked Anim Graphs
  • UE5's New Retargeting System (with IK Rigs and IK Retargeters)
  • Leg IK with Control Rigs
  • Visual Effect Creation in Niagara
  • Enhanced Input System
  • UMG (Unreal Motion Graphics)
  • Static Lighting and Lightmass Importance Volume
  • Post-Process Effects

This project solidified my understanding of Unreal Engine 5's capabilities and provided valuable insights into the game development process. Play the prototype on itch.io to see my initial UE5 learning progress. itch.io
